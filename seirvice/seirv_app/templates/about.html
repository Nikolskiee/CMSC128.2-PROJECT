About SEIR-V

The SEIR-V project, A collaboration between De La Salle Medical and Health Sciences Institute and University of the Philippines- Manila, is a predictive calculating tool that aims to predict the trend of a potential epidemic. We aim to equip our health professionals to be prepared for epidemics that could be as dangerous as COVID-19 surge that started in 2020.
